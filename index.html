Hello,

This section is about the page taking my course at bitwise. I enjoy the animation portion of CSS. The course was a little challenging at first. Then the pieces fell together.

As you can see above, we have the opening Logo, stolen from Nintendo. Side note do not sue me. The landing page Hero Image/Jumbotron is taken from legend of Zelda as well. I wanted to play around with the different Animations.

Page 2

Beginning with my favorite part, "The Waterfall." I have imaged it to change colors as it goes down but did not want to put too much into it. I have copied the original Waterfall and copied it several times. It is three times its length. There is a loop where the image slides down for a few seconds and reverts to its original position.

Next up is the Lightning affects, with several hours of breaking everything else. I used the "filter" thing. Sepia with Brightness. Timed it to about ten secs for every flash.

I just did not know what kind of background I wanted. Thought of a zombie "Legend of Zelda" text, with little droplets of blood. Then having the river turn red after a few seconds. It just seemed like too much work. Got into the SVG and was going to use that to add the dripping effects. The clouds themselves were SVG, I did not create them. There were a few websites that assisted in creating the SVG. I opted out for just coping it.
